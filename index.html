Pokedata

The Pokedata is a modified version of the Pokedex containing all the little bits data you need to know about Pokemon. The Pokedata contains everything including their names, Pokedex numbers, Gender Ratio, Egg groups, Moves, Abilities, Typing, we have everything you need to know about Pokemon from Generation 1 to Generation 9.

The Pokedex's

The Pokedex is a collection of all the information gathered on about Pokemon, there are 2 variations of Pokedexes, the regional dex based on the pokemon available in the region and the national dex based on the worldwide order of pokemon
